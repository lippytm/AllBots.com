# AllBots.com Offer Path

## Core offer position

`AllBots.com` helps organize bots by role so every bot has a clear job, a next step, and a measurable business outcome.

## Free offer

**Bot System Mapping Worksheet**

A worksheet for identifying existing bots, needed bots, missing workflows, and business outcomes.

## Lead magnet

**AllBots Role Map Template**

Helps define:

- Lead Bot.
- FAQ Bot.
- Sales Bot.
- Support Bot.
- Affiliate Bot.
- Follow-Up Bot.
- Training Bot.
- CRM/GitHub tracking.

## Service offers

### Bot System Map

Map the complete bot system before building more automations.

### Bot Management Planning Session

Review current tools, workflows, gaps, and priority bot roles.

### Multi-Bot Implementation Roadmap

Create a phased roadmap for building and connecting bot roles.

### Monthly Bot System Optimization

Review performance, improve bot roles, add follow-up logic, and track outcomes.

## CTA ladder

1. Map Your Bot System.
2. Complete the Bot System Mapping Worksheet.
3. Request a Bot System Map.
4. Build the Multi-Bot Implementation Roadmap.

## Compliance notes

Do not claim bots guarantee sales, leads, income, or business results. Bot systems should support workflows and include human review where needed.
