## Section subheadline

## Section copy

AllBots.com organizes bots by role so every bot has a clear job, a next step, and a measurable business outcome. Instead of building random automations, map the system first.

The goal is to help businesses understand what each bot does, how each bot connects to the next step, and how the system supports leads, support, sales, education, affiliate offers, and follow-up.

- Sales Bot routing.
- Support Bot triage.
- Affiliate Bot disclosure and routing.
- Follow-Up Bot workflows.
- Training Bot education systems.
