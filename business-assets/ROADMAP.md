# AllBots.com Business Asset Roadmap

## Role

`AllBots.com` is the bot management system concept for organizing multiple bots by role, workflow, business outcome, CRM tag, and follow-up path.

## Primary CTA

**Map Your Bot System**

## Website destination

`/roadmap#allbots-map`

## CRM tag

`AllBots System Map`

## Roadmap phases

### Phase 1: Define Bot Roles

Identify the major bot roles: Lead Bot, FAQ Bot, Sales Bot, Support Bot, Affiliate Bot, Follow-Up Bot, Training Bot, and Admin/Tracking Bot.

### Phase 2: Map the System

Connect each bot role to a source, job, next step, human handoff, and measurable business outcome.

### Phase 3: Create Canva AllBots Assets

Create a bot management dashboard card, bot role map, service menu, Instagram carousel, LinkedIn graphic, and website banner.

### Phase 4: Publish the AllBots Map Website Section

Connect the Canva card to `/roadmap#allbots-map` or a dedicated AllBots mapping page.

### Phase 5: Add CRM Tracking

Track AllBots interest, existing bots, desired bot roles, workflow gaps, and consulting opportunities.

### Phase 6: Build the Bot Management Offer

Create a free worksheet, paid bot system map, planning session, and implementation roadmap.

### Phase 7: Expand Into Platform Concept

Develop AllBots.com as a future platform or service hub for bot management, dashboards, workflows, and automation systems.

## Best practices

- Define bot roles before building more tools.
- Keep each bot focused on one clear job.
- Track business outcomes, not just conversations.
- Connect every bot to a next step.
- Include human handoff where needed.
- Use GitHub as the source of truth for bot system maps and roles.
