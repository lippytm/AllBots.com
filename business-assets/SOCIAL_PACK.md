## Facebook post

One bot can help. A system of bots can become a business operation.

The concept behind AllBots.com is to organize bots by role: lead bot, sales bot, support bot, FAQ bot, affiliate bot, follow-up bot, and training bot.

The point is not to make things complicated. The point is to know what each bot is supposed to do and how it helps the business make or save money.

CTA: Map Your Bot System.

## LinkedIn post

As businesses adopt more AI and chat automation, bot management becomes an operational challenge.

AllBots.com is a platform concept for organizing bots by role: lead capture, FAQ support, sales routing, affiliate recommendations, follow-up, support, and training.

The business value comes from clarity. Each bot should have a job, a measurable outcome, and a connection to the larger website, CRM, or follow-up workflow.

## Best practices

- Use the Canva AllBots dashboard card image.
- Keep the message about organization and clarity.
- Avoid making the system sound too complicated.
- Ask people what bots they already use.
- Track comments, DMs, clicks, and mapping requests.
