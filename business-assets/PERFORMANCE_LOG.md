## What to review weekly

- Which platform produced the most AllBots interest?
- Did people understand the difference between one bot and a bot system?
- What bot roles came up most often?
- Did anyone ask for a system map or planning session?
- Does the Canva card need fewer or clearer bot role labels?

## Improvement prompt

```text
Review this AllBots.com performance log and suggest improvements to the AllBots card, CTA, website section, bot role map, social posts, intake questions, and follow-up offer. Goal: increase qualified bot system mapping requests.
```

## Best practices

- Track existing bots and desired bot roles.
- Turn repeated bot roles into service menu items.
- Improve the role map if people are confused.
- Keep the system organized, not overwhelming.
